Pro Tips for Picking Your Song

A few things experienced karaoke hosts know that most singers don't:

  • Pick songs in your range — a song you can actually hit beats an impressive song you can't
  • Know the verses, not just the chorus — most people choke on verses they've never sung aloud
  • Energy beats perfection — commitment and showmanship get more cheers than technique
  • Singalongs save you — the crowd carrying the chorus makes every performance better
  • Don't pick a song from the past 6 months — newer songs often have unfamiliar verses
