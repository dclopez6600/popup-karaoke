Holiday Karaoke FAQ

How far in advance should I book for a holiday party? +

November and December are our busiest months — some weekends book 6–8 weeks out. If you have a specific holiday date, reach out as early as possible. Fridays and Saturdays in December go especially fast.

Do you have Christmas and holiday songs? +

Yes — hundreds of holiday classics plus 61,000+ other songs. Mariah Carey, Michael Bublé, Bing Crosby, Wham!, Brenda Lee, and everything in between. We can also pull up non-holiday songs throughout the night for guests who want variety.

Can you do both dinner and an after-party? +

Absolutely. We can run background karaoke during dinner and then shift to full hosted mode for the after-party. Just tell us your timeline when you book and we'll plan around it.

What if some people don't want to sing? +

Nobody has to sing — karaoke is just as fun to watch. Our host keeps the whole crowd engaged through crowd interaction and themed games. The people who swear they won't sing are usually up by 10 PM.

Do you serve Chicago suburbs for holiday parties? +

Yes — we cover all of Chicagoland including south and southwest suburbs, plus all of Northwest Indiana. Travel fees may apply depending on distance from our NW Indiana base.
