Wedding Karaoke FAQ

How does karaoke work at a wedding reception? +

We integrate seamlessly around your wedding timeline. Our host coordinates with your planner and DJ to find the best window — typically after dinner service, during the after-party, or cocktail hour. Guests sign up to sing, we manage the queue, and your host keeps the energy up between performers. It never feels chaotic — just fun and memorable.

Will karaoke work with our DJ or band? +

Absolutely. We work alongside your DJ or band — not instead of them. Karaoke typically fills a specific window (cocktail hour, after first dances, late-night after-party) while your DJ handles the rest of the night. We coordinate timing before the event so everything flows smoothly.

Can we request songs in advance? +

Yes. When you book, we set up a request list so you and your guests can submit songs ahead of time. Couples often pre-load 20–30 must-plays so the night hits all the right moments — from the couple's song to the groomsmen's choice to grandma's favorite.

How far in advance should we book? +

Summer and fall wedding weekends fill up 4–8 weeks out. We recommend booking as soon as you have your date confirmed — especially for Saturday evenings between May and October. Have a shorter timeline? Reach out anyway and we'll do our best to make it work.

What if our venue has noise restrictions? +

Just let us know the venue rules and we'll work within them. We've performed in spaces with strict decibel limits, no drilling, outdoor-only sound restrictions, and more. We can also provide a certificate of insurance if your venue requires one.

Do you provide a certificate of insurance? +

Yes — many wedding venues require a COI. Just let us know the venue's requirements when you book and we'll have everything ready before the event day.
