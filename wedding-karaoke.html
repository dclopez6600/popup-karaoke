Mobile Karaoke for Weddings

Your wedding reception is the one event where every person in the room — grandparents, groomsmen, flower girls, coworkers — needs to have a great time. Karaoke does that. It gets people on their feet, breaks the ice between families meeting for the first time, and creates moments nobody expected.

PopUp Karaoke handles everything — professional host, full PA system, stage lighting, two wireless microphones, and a library of 61,000+ songs covering every decade and genre. You just supply the singers.

What We Bring to Your Wedding

  • Experienced wedding host who can read a room — keeping energy up without disrupting toasts, first dances, or dinner service
  • Concert-quality sound scaled to your venue size — from backyard tents to banquet halls
  • LED stage lighting that transforms any space into a performance moment
  • Two wireless microphones for duets, group songs, and surprise moments
  • 61,000+ songs — pop, country, R&B, classics, Latin, hip-hop. Something for every generation in the room
  • Seamless transitions around your DJ set, toasts, cake cutting, and other reception events
  • Full setup and teardown — we arrive early, leave after cleanup

Wedding Events We Cover

  • Wedding receptions (full evening or just the after-party)
  • Rehearsal dinners with a fun, relaxed karaoke set
  • Bachelorette & bachelor parties (private or at a bar)
  • Day-after brunch entertainment
  • Engagement parties and anniversary celebrations
  • Combined birthday/wedding celebration events
