Our Story

More Than Just Karaoke

Established in 2020, PopUp Karaoke is a proudly veteran-owned mobile karaoke and event entertainment service for Northwest Indiana and Chicagoland. What started as a hobby grew into the region's premier full-service karaoke experience.

We specialize in high-energy fun for every occasion — corporate parties, team-building, weddings, birthdays, bars and more. Don't just book a booth — book an unforgettable show!

Simple Process

How It Works

From first message to mic drop — six easy steps to your show.

1

Request a Quote

Fill out the booking form with your event date, type, and guest count. Takes under 2 minutes.

2

Get Your Quote

We review your details and send a custom quote — usually within 24 hours. Always free, no obligation.

3

Lock In Your Date

Accept the quote and pay a deposit via our secure Square link to hold your spot on the calendar.

4

Pre-Event Coordination

We confirm setup details, song requests, and any venue requirements (insurance cert, decibel limits, etc.).

5

We Show Up Early

We arrive ahead of your guests, set up the full rig — sound, lights, mics, song display — and do a sound check.

6

Sing All Night

Your guests pick songs and get on the mic. We run the show from start to finish — you just have a great time.
