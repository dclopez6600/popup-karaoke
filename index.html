Our Story


History - Established in 2020, we started PopUp Karaoke as a hobby and it has since evolved into a full service event, with some of the best professional equipment and over 58,000 songs to choose from.

Specialty - PopUp Karaoke is a Veteran owned karaoke service that’s available for Birthdays, Bars/Breweries, Private, Public, and Corporate events! With over 58,000 songs we’ll keep you singing all night long! When you hire us, we bring all of our professional grade equipment, all you need is your voice. Are you a bar/brewery or hosting a public event? We include social media content in our package to promote your event at no additional cost. Need a little wind up/down music? We bring a variety of music to play before or after your karaoke session. Please contact us with any questions or to learn more about our services.
