Corporate Karaoke FAQ

How does karaoke work as a team building activity? +

Corporate karaoke removes hierarchy and gives everyone a shared moment. Our host manages the queue, encourages participation without putting anyone on the spot, and sets a pace that fits your group. Choose from casual open-mic, competitive team tournament, or a full hosted show — we adapt to your company culture.

What if some employees don't want to sing? +

Nobody has to sing. Karaoke works just as well as a spectator sport. Our host keeps everyone engaged whether they're on the mic or cheering from the crowd. We've found the most hesitant people often end up going up by the end of the night — but there's never pressure.

Can you handle 100+ employees? +

Yes — we've run events for 200+ guests. Our PA scales to large venues and our host is experienced at managing long queues and keeping large crowds engaged. Team tournament formats work especially well for bigger groups.

Do you provide a certificate of insurance? +

Yes — we can provide a COI for any venue that requires one. Just share the venue's insurance requirements when you book and we'll have everything ready before your event.

How early should we book for our holiday party? +

November–December books up fast — sometimes 6–8 weeks out. For other times of year, 2–3 weeks is usually enough. If you have a hard date, reach out as early as possible to lock it in.

Can we do a karaoke team competition? +

It's one of our most popular corporate formats. Departments go head-to-head in bracket-style rounds, our host acts as emcee and scorekeeper, and the crowd votes on winners. It adds friendly competition and gives quieter employees a reason to cheer loudly.
