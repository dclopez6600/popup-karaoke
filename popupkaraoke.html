Our Story

More Than Just Karaoke

Established in 2020, PopUp Karaoke started as a hobby and evolved into the premier mobile karaoke and event entertainment service for Northwest Indiana and Chicagoland.

We specialize in high-energy fun for all occasions—from corporate parties and team-building events to weddings and birthday celebrations. Don't just book a booth—book an unforgettable show!
