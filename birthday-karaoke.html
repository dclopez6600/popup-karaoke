The Birthday Party Entertainment That Actually Lands

Birthday parties are personal. The stakes are different than a bar night or a corporate event — someone's being celebrated, and the people who showed up came to make them feel special. That context changes everything about how we run the show. When you book karaoke for a birthday, we're not just running a song queue — we're building a night around the guest of honor.

We've done 21sts that turned into all-night dance parties. We've done 50ths where the honoree's 75-year-old mother ended up with a standing ovation. The common thread in every one of those nights: a host who read the room, adapted, and made the birthday person the center of the universe. That's what PopUp Karaoke brings to every event.

Frequently Asked Questions — Birthday Party Karaoke

Can you do karaoke at a birthday party?

Absolutely — birthday parties are one of our most popular bookings. We bring everything: PA system, microphones, LED lighting, a 61,000+ song library, and a live host to run the show.

What if the birthday person is shy about singing?

Extremely common — and totally fine. A good karaoke host knows how to ease people in. By the time the mic is in front of the guest of honor, the crowd is already loose and supportive. We've turned hundreds of "I would never sing" people into first-timers.

Can I request specific songs in advance?

Yes — we love this. Share the birthday person's favorites before the event and we'll make sure they're ready to go. We can even plan a surprise opening medley of their songs to kick off the night.

Do you bring all the equipment?

Yes, completely turnkey. We bring speakers, microphones, lighting, a lyrics display, and the full song library. You don't need to rent or provide anything.

How far in advance should I book?

For Friday and Saturday nights — especially in summer — we recommend booking 3–6 weeks out. We often accommodate last-minute requests when availability allows. Call us and we'll check right away.
