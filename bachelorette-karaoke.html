Bachelorette Karaoke FAQ

Can you set up at a private home or Airbnb? +

Yes — private home bachelorette karaoke is one of our most popular setups. We bring the full rig wherever there's space for your group. We just need access to standard electrical outlets and address details when you book.

Can we build a custom song list for the bride? +

Absolutely — when you book, we set up a request link you can share with the whole group. The bride's favorites get priority, and everyone else can add their picks ahead of time so the night runs smooth from song one.

How many guests can you handle? +

Anywhere from 10 to 100+ guests. Our setup scales with your space — intimate home rig for smaller groups, full PA for larger venue events. Just share your headcount when you inquire and we'll recommend the right configuration.

Can we also book karaoke for the wedding? +

Many bachelorette clients become wedding clients — and we'd love that. We run wedding reception karaoke across the same area. See our Wedding Karaoke page for everything that's included.

Do you serve Chicago and suburbs? +

Yes — all of Chicagoland including south and southwest suburbs, plus all of Northwest Indiana. Travel fees may apply based on your distance from our NW Indiana base. Reach out and we'll give you a specific quote.
